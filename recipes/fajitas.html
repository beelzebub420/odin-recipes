Fajitas


The best Fajitas you'll ever eat

    Ingridients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Large red onion Onion (Chopped)
  • Mixed Peppers (Chopped)
  • BBQ Fajita Rub
  • Sour Cream
  • Salsa
  • Tortillas

    Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan
  2. Add Chicken and cook untill white
  3. Add Onion
  4. Add Peppers
  5. Throw in BBQ rub
  6. Continue to simmer until chicken is cooked through

Heat your totrtillas in the microwave for 1 minute whilst chicken mix simmers

Serve chick mix in a big bowl. Take a tortilla and apply a generous serving of salsa and spread it across the whole wrap with a spoon. Apply sour cream and guacamole. Add a couple of spoonfuls of chicken mix to your wrap through the centre like rolling a cigarette or a joint

    How To Wrap

  • Bring the right side of the wrap to the centre
  • Bring the bottom of the wrap up a third of the way
  • Bring the left side of the wrap over all the way
