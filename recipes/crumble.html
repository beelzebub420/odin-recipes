Apple Crumble


  • Apples
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • cinnamon
  • Oats
  • Caster sugar
  • demera sugar

Steps

  1. Add flour, oats, butter and sugar into bowl
  2. use fingers to mix into a fine crumb
  3. Peel cooking apples
  4. Chop apples into chunks
  5. Place into a pan (with half a pint of water) and place on the hob
  6. Add castor sugar to sweeten
  7. Bring to a boil until apples have softened

once the apples have softened, pour them into your ceramic bowl. Pour the crumb over the apples generously. Sprinkle with a little bit of demerera sugar for carmalised layer.

Bake for 30 minutes

serve your apple crumble with a portion of double cream
