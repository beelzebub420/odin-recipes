Lasagna


How to make the best lasagana that you've ever eaten. With its cheesey goodnes and layers of soft pasta sheets this beef masterpiece is a great source of protein and an amazing italian dinner.

    Ingridients

  • Beef Mince Meat
  • Olive Oil
  • Large White Onion (Diced)
  • Mixed Peppers (Diced)
  • Celery
  • Tinned Tomatoes
  • Italian Mixed Herbs
  • Garlic (Minced)
  • Pasata
  • Tomato Puree
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Bechamel Sauce
  • Pasta Sheets
  • Cheese (Cheddar, Parmesan & Mozerella

    Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan
  2. Add minced beef and begin to brown
  3. Add garlic (minced)
  4. Add diced onion & Celery
  5. Simmer on a low heat until sauteed
  6. Add mixed peppers (Diced)
  7. Add in tinned tomatoes, tomato puree (1Tbps) & pasata
  8. Throw in italian mixed herbs and a splash of Worcestershire sauce
  9. Simmer on a low heat until everything has softened (Approx 1 hour)

Whilst your sauce is simmering, prepare the ceramic deep tray and begin to layer pasta sheets.

Take your sauce off of the heat and using a big spoon, gradually spread the sauce on the first layer of pasta sheets. Add another layer of pasta sheets on top of the sauce and repeat Until you have 4 juicy layers of pasta. To finish off your creamy lasagane, apply 1 final layer of sheets and add you Bechamel sauce. Take a gernerous helping of mature grated cheddar and sprinkle evenly. Add in a small amount of Mozerella and Parmesanand finish with cracked black peppers

  • Bake for 35 minutes until bubbling and golden brown.
  • Allow to sit for 30 minutes before serving
